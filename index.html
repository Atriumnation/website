Artiumnation is an Indonesian cultural performance that is held to celebrate the 12th Anniversary of Saman Melbourne and to introduce Saman Melbourne to a larger scope. This event will be held on March 11th, 2017 at Melbourne Town Hall. Artiumnation in Indonesia means Seni Budaya Tanah Air.

With the theme " Fly to Indonesia",
Saman Melbourne will present the nuances of on-board flight with performances from Sabang to Merauke accompanied by a magnificent traditional music, mini drama, acapella and famous guest stars.
