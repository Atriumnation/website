Saman Melbourne is a dance group based in Melbourne. Our Members are mix of Indonesian, Australian and other nationalities who simply love Saman dance, Indonesian cultures and arts. We aim to become the ambassador to introduce the beauty of Indonesia to wider Australian community through perfomances, regular workshops and also workshops to local schools and universities.

OUR MISSION

To make each of us an ambassador to introduce the beauty of Indonesia.

To introduce Saman as widely as possible in Victoria, especially in the city of Melbourne.


Also to enable regeneration of Saman by embracing membership that is open to everyone regardless their age, nationality or profession.
